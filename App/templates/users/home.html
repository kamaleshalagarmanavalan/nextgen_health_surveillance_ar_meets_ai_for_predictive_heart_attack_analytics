Our Story

Heart Detection and Prevention Project

This project aims to detect heart conditions such as Heart Block (HB), Myocardial Infarction (MI), Post-Myocardial Infarction (PMI), and Normal heart conditions using deep learning models. Through cutting-edge AI technology, we strive to help medical professionals diagnose heart-related issues more efficiently, potentially saving countless lives.

Our Mission

To improve early detection and prevention of life-threatening heart conditions using AI-based solutions.
